About Me

I am a:

  • 2000s kid
  • Computer science student at University of California, San Diego
  • Served as a software engineer intern, IT intern, and tutor
  • Favorite Latin quote: Dum spiro, spero.

Since I started learning to code, my goal has been to make technology cooler, more accessible, and something everyone can interact with.

I believe that when people use technology to do amazing things, they build a relationship of sorts with that technology, and I want to help others make the most out of those relationships to bring their visions to reality.

I am a senior Computer Science student at UC San Diego, but more importantly I've been a team leader, a co-founder, a collaborator, an innovator, and a passionate coder. My career journey has enabled me to modernize the IT service desk of Anheuser-Busch, the world’s largest brewing company, teach hundreds of students from 15 nations how to code, lead one of UCSD’s most popular business courses, and develop relevant and impactful projects.

I am comfortable with Java, Python, and the HTML/CSS/JS trio and I have experience with Agile development. With me in your organization, you don’t just get a fresh engineer; You get someone who knows how to communicate ideas with diverse audiences and is willing to solve problems that others would not address.
I am open to offers to work.

Under the UC Education Abroad Program I had the privilege of being able to study in Yonsei University as an international student.


While studying abroad, I also joined Yonsei Taekwondo's international student division and was able to obtain an orange belt by December 2022.


I also joined an expat church and was able to not only learn more about Korean life and culture, but also better understand the challenges involved in living and working long-term in a new country.


This experience may have been short, but it shaped me into someone more willing to make mistakes, more ambitious in my goals, and more open to listening to others' experiences.
